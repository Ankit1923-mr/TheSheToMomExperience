Daily Wisdom (Vedic & Ayurvedic Resources)

These resources are curated, expert-approved content to support your physical and mental healing journey, complementing your personalized AI Care Plan.

Ahara (Postpartum Diet)

  • **Warming Foods:** Focus on easily digestible grains like *moong dal khichdi* and oats. Avoid raw vegetables and cold drinks.
  • **Healing Fats:** Incorporate *ghee* generously. It helps lubricate joints and supports nerve health post-delivery.
  • **Lactation Support:** Drink *haldi doodh* (turmeric milk) and water with fennel seeds (*saunf*) to aid digestion and milk production.
  • **Recipe Idea:** Try a simple, warming **Ginger-Jaggery *Panjiri*** for energy and strength.

Vihara (Body & Routine)

  • **Rest is Paramount:** Prioritize sleep over household chores. Sleep when the baby sleeps, regardless of the time of day.
  • **Abhyanga (Oil Massage):** Gentle daily self-massage with warm sesame oil (*til tel*) helps stabilize *Vata* (air element) and aids physical recovery.
  • **Gentle Movement:** Start with simple ankle and wrist rotations. Wait 6 weeks (or doctor's approval) for floor exercises.
  • **Warmth:** Avoid cold temperatures. Keep your head and ears covered, even indoors, for the first few weeks.

Mana (Mind & Soul)

  • **Simple Pranayama:** Practice *Anulom Vilom* (alternate nostril breathing) for 5 minutes daily to calm the nervous system and clear the mind.
  • **Mantra:** Repeating a simple, positive mantra like "Om Shanti" can stabilize emotions.
